We are a team from HELOPOLIS ACADEMY, participating in ORCE (A Hands-On Approach to STEM Learning). Our goal is to let the disabled kids (physically challenged) feel included and have fun with the other kids while not feeling that they are a special case to society. So, one of the places where they often feel left out is aquaparks. That's why we have come together—Basma Alaa, Maraim Saber, and Maraim El Balbly—to develop a groundbreaking product that aims to empower and integrate disabled children within aquapark settings.
